We specialize in helping our clients record their music to its fullest potential without breaking the bank.

Recording can be an expensive and unpredictable process. We take the guesswork out of it with our unique approach to music production which delivers professional-quality results on budget and on schedule.

We work in a number of different studio facilities around Los Angeles, each of which differ in size and accommodation of certain types of projects. The largest and most-equipped facilities come at the highest cost. Most projects can be completed start-to-finish at any of these facilities, but because they each have their own strengths and limitations, we’ve found that using different facilities for different tasks leads to excellent results at the best possible price.

How we work

  1. Consultation

    We’ll have a short conversation with you about your goals for your project and your available budget.

  2. Planning

    We’ll create a report detailing several different options for how to approach your project. In most cases, this report will include an option that exactly meets your budget, plus a couple more options above and below, along with detailed pros and cons for each. We’ll discuss and pick the best option for you. This will form the roadmap for the production process.

  3. Production

    Next, we’ll get to work making music! You’ll be able to focus on your music with confidence that your requirements will be met on schedule and within your budget.
